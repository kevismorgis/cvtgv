Cave Club FAQ

What is Cave Club and how do I get in?

Cave Club is a special hang out + acoustic performance + exclusive merch package that's available on some of my tours and takes place before my shows. Tickets are available through my website when tour tickets first go on sale, but they sell out fast!

Can I still get tickets for ur Fall USA Tour Cave Club?

Unfortunately, all tickets for Cave Club on this tour are sold out :(

Is Cave Club available outside the USA?

Not yet! Sorry!

How do I pick up my tickets?

All tickets are available for will call pickup at the box office the night of the show.

What time do we arrive for Cave Club?

Cave Club is usually held about two hours before doors open for the main show, but my Cave Club manager will send u an email with instructions.

I haven't gotten an email yet!

U will get an email to the address used to purchase the tickets at least a few days before the show. If u still don't see the email, be sure to check ur spam/junk folders!

I don't have the ID used to purchase the tickets – what do I do?

Usually a photo of the purchaser's ID will work, but if u are unsure it's best to call the venue and check with them because different venues have different rules.

Can I change the name my tickets are under?

U will need to call Soundrink to do that.

Can I change the size of shirt I asked for?

Since we pre-order the sizes based on ur order, we can't offer any size changes :(
